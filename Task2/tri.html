Sushant Singh Rajput was a popular Indian actor who was born on January 21st, 1986, in Patna, Bihar. He began his career in the entertainment industry as a television actor and gained popularity with his portrayal of Manav in the TV show "Pavitra Rishta".

In 2013, Sushant made his Bollywood debut with the movie "Kai Po Che!", which was a critical and commercial success. He went on to deliver stellar performances in several movies such as "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story", "Chhichhore", "Shuddh Desi Romance" and "PK". He was praised for his dedication to his craft and his ability to bring a unique perspective to his characters.

Apart from his acting career, Sushant was also an avid learner and had a keen interest in science and technology. He was reportedly a voracious reader and a self-taught technologist. He even bought a piece of land on the moon, which made headlines in the media.

Tragically, Sushant passed away on June 14th, 2020, leaving his fans and well-wishers in shock and disbelief. He will always be remembered for his contribution to the Indian film industry and the joy and entertainment he brought to his fans.

In memory of Sushant Singh Rajput, his family and fans have established a foundation called Sushant Singh Rajput Foundation (SSRF), which aims to support young talents in the fields of science, technology, and performing arts. The foundation seeks to promote Sushant's vision of empowering and inspiring the youth to achieve their dreams.

CAREER

Sushant Singh Rajput did his schooling at St. Karen's High School in Patna and then at Kulachi Hansraj Model School in New Delhi. Sushant Singh Rajput was good at studies and cleared 11 engineering entrance examinations in his entire life.

In 2002, he lost his mother and his entire family moved from Patna to Delhi. One of his sisters, Mitu Singh is a state-level cricketer.

In 2003, he secured the seventh rank in the Delhi College of Engineering Entrance Exam and got admitted in B.E. Mechanical Engineering. Sushant was also a National Olympiad Winner in Physics. At Delhi College of Engineering, he actively participated in theatre. He drops out of college to pursue his acting career.
